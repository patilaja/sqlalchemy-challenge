List of all available Hawaii API routes (Web page output)
  • List of precipitations for last year (Get a year ago date from the max date):
    /api/v1.0/precipitation
  • List stations from the dataset:
    /api/v1.0/stations
  • Dates and temperature observations of the most active station for the last year (Get a year ago date from the max date):
    /api/v1.0/tobs
  • List of tmin, tmax, tavg for the dates greater than or equal to the date (Year-Month-Day format):
    /api/v1.0/2017-01-01
  • List of tmin, tmax, tavg for the dates range - start date and end date (Year-Month-Day format):
    /api/v1.0/2017-01-01/2017-01-07
